About Me

Since graduating I have worked as an IT consultant at RSM US LLP. I have experience working with servers both physical and virtual. I have a CCNA certification and have plenty of hands on experience with Cisco routers, switches and firewalls.

I intend to add to my technology skillset by attending a coding bootcamp at Southern Methodist Uiversity and building on my existing web development knowledge.
